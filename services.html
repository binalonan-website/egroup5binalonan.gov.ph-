Our Services

How Can We Help You?

The Municipal Government of Binalonan is committed to delivering efficient, accessible, and quality services to every resident and business in our community.

Civil Registry

Processing of birth, marriage, and death certificates. Registration of civil status documents for all residents of Binalonan.

Walk-in Available

Business Permits

Application and renewal of business permits and licenses. We support local entrepreneurs and new business establishments.

Annual Renewal

Health Services

Free medical consultations, medicines, and health programs for all Binalonan residents through our Rural Health Unit.

Free for Residents

Education & Scholarships

Scholarship programs, school supplies assistance, and support for students from kindergarten to college level.

Apply Now Open

Social Welfare

Assistance programs for senior citizens, persons with disabilities, indigent families, and solo parents in our community.

MSWD Office

Public Safety

24/7 disaster preparedness, emergency response, and coordination with local police for the safety of all residents.

24/7 Available
