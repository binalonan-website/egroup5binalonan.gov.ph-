About Binalonan

A Community of Heritage & Progress

Binalonan is a 1st class municipality in the province of Pangasinan, Philippines. Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and progressive development, it stands as one of the most dynamic municipalities in Eastern Pangasinan.

The municipality is the birthplace of Carlos Bulosan, a celebrated Filipino-American author and labor activist, whose legacy continues to inspire the community's values of dignity, resilience, and progress.

With a growing economy anchored in agriculture, trade, and emerging industries, Binalonan is well on its way to becoming a model of sustainable local governance in the region.
